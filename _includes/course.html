Obstacle Avoidance Trajectory Optimization
  • report
Motion planning is an essential part for autonomous driving vehicles. One interesting subject within this area is planning a trajectory that safely overtakes an obstaclein the front. Our project solved this trajectory planning problem with various optimization based methods including iterative linearquadratic regulator (iLQR), genetic algorithm (GA), and interior point methods(IPMs).
Policy Gradient Tutorial
  • material
We gave a oral tutorial about Policy Gradient theorem. We divided the theorem into two main parts: policy gradient for episodic tasks and continuing problems. We included topics like:
Why policy gradient?
What is the advantage of the policy gradient?
How does the policy gradient perform? etc.
We covered some common algorithms,
such as REINFORCE: Monte Carlo Policy Gradient,
REINFORCE with Baseline,
One-step TD Actor-Critic,
Q Actor-Critic,
Advantage Actor-Critic,
Natural Policy Gradient.
